Frequently Asked Questions

Installation takes under two minutes with zero tools. Simply unscrew your existing faucet aerator, attach the included adapter, and twist the brume filter into place. It fits most standard round-aerator faucets right out of the box.

Our advanced 5-stage filtration removes chlorine, heavy metals (lead, mercury, copper), sediment, rust, and other impurities that dry out your skin and strip away your natural glow - while retaining beneficial minerals your body needs.

Each filter cartridge lasts approximately 3 months or 400 litres, depending on your water quality and usage. We recommend replacing it regularly for optimal filtration performance.

brume is compatible with most standard round-aerator faucets. The kit includes multiple adapter sizes to ensure a secure fit. If your faucet has a non-standard or square aerator tip, reach out to our support team and we'll help find a solution.

Simply twist off the bottom housing, remove the old cartridge, drop in the new one, and twist the housing back on. The entire process takes under 30 seconds - no tools or plumber required.
